### Question 9

$\frac{5}{40} = \frac{X}{128}$

$X = \frac{5 \times 128}{40} = \frac{640}{40} = 16 liters$

### Question 11  
Culture : Eman reads 10 pages in 40 minutes. How much time, in hours would it take her to read a book of 120 pages if she reads at the same rate ?  

$\frac{10 \, \text{pages}}{40 \, Minutes} = \frac{120 \, pages}{X}$

$X = \frac{40 \times 120}{10} = \frac{4800}{10} = 480 \,  minutes = \frac{480}{60} = 8 \, hours$

### Question 15  

$$ \frac{1 \, wall}{4 \, hours} \, and \, \frac{1 \, wall}{2 \, hours} $$


**Rate Compcombined** $\frac{1}{4} + \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{4} + \frac{1 \times 2}{2 \times 2} = \frac{1}{4} + \frac{2}{4} = \frac{3}{4}$

**Calculate the time required to paint one wall** $Time = \frac{1}{\frac{3}{4}} = \frac{4}{3} = 1.33 \,Hours = 1.33 \times 60 = 80 \, minutes$
